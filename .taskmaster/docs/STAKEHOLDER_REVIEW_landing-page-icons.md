## Detailed Section Breakdown for Review

### 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)

**Content:**
- Headline: "Get Your Pre-Launch Landing Page Checklist"
- Subheadline: "The 5-step framework 500+ founders used to validate their ideas"
- CTA: "Download Free Checklist ‚Üí"

**Proposed Icons:**
1. **Main Icon:** `FileText` or `ClipboardCheck`
   - **Visual:** Document/checklist representation
   - **Size:** Large (h-12 w-12 on mobile, h-16 w-16 on desktop)
   - **Placement:** Centered above headline
   - **Alternative:** `CheckSquare` if more emphasis on "completion" needed

2. **CTA Button Icon:** `ArrowRight`
   - **Visual:** Forward movement/action
   - **Size:** Small (h-4 w-4)
   - **Placement:** Inside button, after text
   - **Alternative:** `Download` if emphasizing download action

**‚ùì Stakeholder Questions:**
- [ ] Which hero icon feels more appropriate: `FileText` (document-focused) or `ClipboardCheck` (action-focused)?
- [ ] Should the CTA button use `ArrowRight` (forward movement) or `Download` (direct action)?

### 2. Discovery/Process Section

**Content:**
- Title: "Discovery"
- Description: "We kick off with a focused session to understand your idea, target audience, and key assumptions to test"

**Proposed Icon:** `Compass`
- **Visual:** Exploration, navigation, finding direction
- **Size:** Medium-Large (h-8 w-8 to h-10 w-10)
- **Placement:** Above or inline with section title
- **Rationale:** Compass symbolizes discovery, exploration, and guidance‚Äîperfectly aligning with the section's message

**Alternative Options:**
- `Search` - More literal "finding" interpretation
- `Lightbulb` - Emphasizes ideation
- `Target` - Focuses on precision and goals
- `Telescope` - Looking ahead/discovery angle

**‚ùì Stakeholder Questions:**
- [ ] Does `Compass` effectively communicate the "discovery" process?
- [ ] Would any of the alternatives better represent your brand voice?
- [ ] Should this icon have animation on hover (e.g., gentle rotation)?

### 3. Trust Indicators Section

**Content:** Three trust badges to reduce friction and build credibility

**Proposed Icons:**

#### Badge 1: "Used by 100+ founders"
- **Icon:** `Users`
- **Visual:** Community, social proof
- **Color:** Success green or primary brand
- **Alternative:** `UserCheck` (adds checkmark for "verified" feel)

#### Badge 2: "No credit card required"
- **Icon:** `ShieldCheck`
- **Visual:** Security, protection, trust
- **Color:** Success green
- **Alternative:** `Lock` with check overlay (more security-focused)

#### Badge 3: "Instant download"
- **Icon:** `Zap`
- **Visual:** Speed, lightning-fast, instant
- **Color:** Success green or accent
- **Alternative:** `Download` (more literal, less exciting)

**‚ùì Stakeholder Questions:**
- [ ] Do these icons clearly communicate trust and low friction?
- [ ] Should all three badges use the same color (green) or vary by meaning?
- [ ] Is the "instant" messaging better represented by `Zap` or `Download`?

## Design System Questions

### Color Palette
**Proposed:**
- Primary: `#667eea` (Purple)
- Accent: `#764ba2` (Darker purple)
- Success: `#10b981` (Green)
- Warning: `#f59e0b` (Orange)
- Error: `#ef4444` (Red)
- Neutral: `#6b7280` (Gray)

**‚ùì Questions:**
- [ ] Does this align with existing brand colors?
- [ ] Any adjustments needed for accessibility (contrast ratios)?

### Icon Sizing
**Proposed Scale:**
- XS: h-3 w-3 (inline text)
- SM: h-4 w-4 to h-5 w-5 (buttons, badges)
- MD: h-6 w-6 to h-8 w-8 (section icons)
- LG: h-10 w-10 to h-12 w-12 (hero, major sections)
- XL: h-16 w-16 (rare, hero emphasis only)

**‚ùì Questions:**
- [ ] Do these sizes feel appropriate for your target audience (founders)?
- [ ] Should mobile sizes scale down more aggressively?

### Animations
**Proposed:**
- Hover: Subtle scale (110%) or color shift
- Transition: 200ms ease-in-out
- Loading: Rotate animation for spinners
- Entry: Optional fade-in on scroll

**‚ùì Questions:**
- [ ] Should icons have hover animations, or keep them static?
- [ ] Any specific animation preferences (brand personality)?
