## Target Audience Profiles

### V1: Professional Founders
**Mindset**: "I know what I need. Show me the options."
- Time-constrained
- Decision-ready
- Prefers clarity over persuasion
- Responds to features/benefits
- Low tolerance for fluff

**When They Convert**:
- After reviewing packages
- When benefits align with needs
- After seeing examples
- Quick decision cycle (1 visit)

---

### V2: Pain-Aware Founders
**Mindset**: "I've been burned before. Prove this works."
- Previous failed launches
- Data-driven decision maker
- Skeptical of "pretty" solutions
- Responds to problem framing
- Needs proof upfront

**When They Convert**:
- After seeing stats/proof
- When pain points resonate
- After reviewing examples
- Quick-to-medium cycle (1-2 visits)

---

### V3: Trust-Seeking Founders
**Mindset**: "Who are you? Why should I trust you?"
- First-time founders or risk-averse
- Needs comprehensive information
- Values authenticity/transparency
- Responds to personal story
- Needs multiple touchpoints

**When They Convert**:
- After reading full story
- When credentials resonate
- After downloading checklist (lead nurture)
- Medium-to-long cycle (2-4 visits)
